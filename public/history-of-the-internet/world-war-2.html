World War 2

On October 4, 1957, the Soviet Union launched mankind's first ever artificial satellite into space, Sputnik I. They also tested their intercontinental ballistic missile. The US government, on the otherhand, headed by President Dwight Eisenhower started their own military campaign to compete with the U.S.S.R. which fave birth to the Advanced Research Projects Agency (ARPA).

ARPA was the government's research agency for their missiles and military strategies. In 1958, NASA was formed, giving way for ARPA to focus more on computer science and information processing instead of including aeronautics in their field.

Vannevar Bush recognized the idea of managing information and data, he then theorized that could manage it. He called this theoretical machine, the memex.
